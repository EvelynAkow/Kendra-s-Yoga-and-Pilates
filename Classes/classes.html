Classes

What class suits you best?

Hatha Yoga

Stretch and strengthen your body. I modify this class for beginners and advanced students alike with variations for each movement. These dynamic flows will leave you feeling rejuvenated and empowered.

Reformer Pilates

Building a strong core is a must! In this class we utilize exclusive equipment like the Reformer, Spring Board, Ladder Barrel and Exo Chair to build strength, stabilization and control. This class is great for sufferers of low back pain, injury recovery and enhancing athletic performance. Offered as private sessions only.

Yin Yoga

Bring balance to your life through rest. Yin targets the deep connective tissues in the body and increases mobility at the joints. This class is a must for anyone with tight hips! Leave feeling relaxed and refreshed.

Meditation

Reduce stress, learn to ride the waves of your emotions and live more in the moment. In these sessions we focus on breath work, connecting with ourselves and the world around us.
